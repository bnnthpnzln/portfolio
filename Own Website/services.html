SERVICES
We offer a variety of services to keep your pet as healthy as possible. Our services include:

DENTAL CARE
Routine expert cleansing is very important to maintaining your animal’s teeth. We make use of modern and secure ultrasound equipment to thoroughly clean each tooth – above and listed below the gum line. Our professionals polish your pet’s teeth to create a smooth, shiny tooth areas which assist in preventing plaque buildup. We schedule dental cleanings every Tuesday and Thursday morning.

ON-SITE PHARMACY
We stock a wide range of the best veterinary drugs for your animals. If we do not have a particular medication on hand, we can usually special order it and expect it in by the next day, or it can be ordered through our website using Vets 1st Choice.

HOUSE CALLS
Dr. Mascola is glad to schedule house calls if you are unable to bring your pet to the clinic. Typically, house calls are scheduled primarily for euthanasia. However, in limited circumstances, visits for other care can be scheduled.

IMMUNIZATION AND WELLNESS CARE
Prevention of health problems for your pet is our primary goal. We provide Comprehensive physical examinations; internal parasite testing, heartworm and flea control; vaccinations, spaying and neutering; and necessary blood tests if needed.

NUTRITIONAL COUNSELING
Our staff is available to help you choose the right food to keep your animal happy and healthy.

PAIN MANAGEMENT
Animals can have pain just like their owners. Dr. Mascola can provide your animal with the most up to date techniques when your pet requires relief from pain.

RADIOLOGY

SPECIALISTS
Sometimes conditions arise that require veterinarians with specialized training. If your pet’s medical condition requires it, we have veterinary specialists available for consultation. Dr. Jane Toomey and Dr. Michael Stone are boarded in internal medicine and provide mobile ultrasound services for Petcare, as needed. And, Dr. Michael Frost provides mobile surgical services, as needed.
