SERVICES
We offer a variety of services to keep your pet as healthy as possible. Our services include:

DENTAL CARE
Routine expert cleansing is very important to maintaining your animal’s teeth. We make use of modern and secure ultrasound equipment to thoroughly clean each tooth – above and listed below the gum line. Our professionals polish your pet’s teeth to create a smooth, shiny tooth areas which assist in preventing plaque buildup. We schedule dental cleanings every Tuesday and Thursday morning.

ON-SITE PHARMACY
We stock a wide range of the best veterinary drugs for your animals. If we do not have a particular medication on hand, we can usually special order it and expect it in by the next day, or it can be ordered through our website using Vets 1st Choice.
